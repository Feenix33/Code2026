Pasta Pomodoro

1/4 c + 1 Tbsp olive oil, divided
1 shallot, chopped
1 1/2 tsp water
1/2 tsp salt
1 can 28oz San Marzano tomatoes, pureed
5 chopped basil leaves
12oz spaghetti
1 Tbsp Parmigiano Reggiano

1. In lg skillet, cook shallot in 1/4c oil over med until brown & soft.Add water, salt, tomatoes.
2. Cook sauce until thinkens, 15m. Stir in basil.
3. Cooks spaghetti in separate pot.
4. Add spaghetti to sauce + 1 T oil + cheese. 
5. Serve. Garnish w/additional basil & cheese
