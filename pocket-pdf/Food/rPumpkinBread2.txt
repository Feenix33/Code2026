2 cups granulated sugar
1 (15-ounce) can pumpkin puree (about 2 cups)
1/2 cup vegetable or canola oil
3 large eggs
1 table pumpkin pie spice
1 1/2 tea baking powder
3/4 tea baking soda
1/2 tea salt
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup roasted, salted pumpkin seeds (aka pepitas)

Pie Spice Sub 
1 tea ground cinnamon
1 tea ground nutmeg
1/2 tea ground cloves
1/4 tea ground ginger.

Oven 350F.
Coat a 9x5-inch loaf pan with cooking spray
Whisk sugar, pumpkin, oil, and eggs until smooth and combined.
Add the pumpkin pie spice, baking powder, baking soda, and salt, and whisk to combine.
Add the flour and fold it in with a rubber spatula until just combined.
Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread into an even layer.
Sprinkle with the pumpkin seeds, then gently press them into the batter.
Bake 65 to 75 minutes.
Cool 10 min in the pan, then transfer to a cooling rack and let cool completely.
