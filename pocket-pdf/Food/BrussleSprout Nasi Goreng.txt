Brussels Sprout Nasi Goreng
1 3/4 c jasmine rice
3T canola oil
1 med red onion chopped
4 cloves garlic, crushed
3 bird's eye chilies, finely chopped
1 3/4 lbs brussel sprouts
2 T tomato paste
2 T kecap manis (sweet soy, maybe use oyster sauce?)
1 1/4 t salt
2 T soy sauce
2 T white wine vinegar
2 T toasted sesame oil
1 t sugar

1. Cook rice
2. nasi goreng base: heat oil lg fry pan med heat
3. fry onion 5m, add garlic and 2/3 chilies cook 2m
4. add all but 2 handful of sprouts, fry 8m leaving undisturbed couple min at a time
5. add paste, kecap, salt, T soy & vinegar; cook 5m remove from heat
6. marinate sprouts: put remain sprouts in bowl w/1T soy & vinegar plus sesame, sugar, remain chilies
7. put cooked sprouts back on heat, fold in rice and mix until heated through
8. trans to a big platter and put marinated sprouts on top
