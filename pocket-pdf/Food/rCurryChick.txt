1 tbs olive oil
1 onion diced
3 clove garlic
2 Tbs curry powder
1 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp paprika
3/4 tsp salt
1 can crushed tomatoes 15ox
1 can coconut milk
1 can chickpeas, drained
1 Tbs almond butter
1 handful spinach

1 Med heat cook onion in oil ~3m
2 Add garlic spices. stir until fragrant ~5m
3 Add tomatoes, milk, chickpeas, butter
4 Bring to simmer, cook 6-8m

serve w/naan or rice
