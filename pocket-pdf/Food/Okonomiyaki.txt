Napa Cabbage Okonomiyaki

Okonomiyaki
1 1/4 c flour
1 1/4 t salt
4 eggs
4c shredded Napa
6 g onions, fine chop, white & green separated
2 T oil

Okonomiyaki Sauce
2 1/2 T ketchup
2 1/2 T A1
2 1/2 T date syrup

1. Whisk flour, salt, eggs, 2/3 c water until smooth
2. Add cabage and green onion whites, mix
3. Make sauce: mix all sauce ingredients
4. Heat 1T oil in 8-in non-stick med high heat
5. Add half batter, 3-4 min flip other side
6. Serve w/sauce on top, mayo on top, sprinkle w/crispy fried onions and green onions
