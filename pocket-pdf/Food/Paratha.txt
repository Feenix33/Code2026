Mashed Potato Paratha
w/ Lemon Pickle

Pickle
2 lemons
2 T canola
1/2 t black mustard seeds
1 clove garlic thin slice
1 Fresno or other ed chilie fine chop
1/2 t salt

1. Trip top & bottom of 1 lemon
2. cut into quarters, then thin slice
3. Slices in a bowl, add juice of other lemon
4. heat oil in lidded fry pan, low heat
5. add mustard seeds, garlic
6. Garlic turns gold add chilie, slices, joice, salt
7. Cover cook 5min
8. Remove lid cook 2-3 min more
9. Off heat, cool

Parathas
1 lb russet, peeled cut 1.5 in chunks
2 T canola
1 t salt
1.5 in ginger, peeled grated
1.5 serrano chillie, fine chop
3 T fresh cilanto fine chop
1 med red onion, fine dice
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 2/3 c all-purp flour
yogurt

1. Cook potatoes in pan of water 12 min until tender
2. Drain, dry, put back in pan
3. Add oil and mash
4. add salt, ginger chilies, cilantor, onion, cumin & mix
5. Add flour and knead until uniform
6. Cut into 8 pieces
7. Roll piece inot 5 1/2 in cicle
8. Heat 1t oil in non-stick pan until hot
9. Cook 1.5 min /side
10. Serve w/lemons & yogurt
