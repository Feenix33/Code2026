Honey Basil Dressing
1/3 c apple cider vinegar
1 clove garlic 
1/2 t kosher salt
2 Tbl honey
1 oz fresh basil leaves, about 2 cups lightly packed
1 cup olive oil

Blend vinegar, garlic, salt, and honey
Add basil and process until smooth and bright green.
With blender running on low speed, slowly drizzle in oil until emulsified.
