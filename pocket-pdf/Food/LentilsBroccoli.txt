Braised Lentils w/Brocolli Rabe

4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
1 medium onion (or shallots), finely chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1 1/2 cups green French lentils
1 tablespoon tomato paste
3 cups stock (chicken or vegetable), plus some extra if needed
1 large bunch (or 2 small bunches) broccoli rabe
1 clove garlic, sliced
1 large tomato, chopped
1/2 cup heavy cream
1 to 2 tablespoons butter
Handful fresh basil leaves, roughly torn
Salt and pepper to taste

Heat 2 Tbsp of the oil in a heavy-bottom saucepan.
Sweat onions over low heat for about 8m until translucent, season w/salt & pepper.
Add minced garlic & cook another minute.
Add lentils, tomato paste & a splash of stock.
Increase heat to med & stir until stock absorbed.
Continue adding stock & stirring until lentils are tender, about 30 min.
Bring a separate pot of water to boil & prepare lg ice bath.
Drop the broccoli in the boiling water & cook 1-2 min.
Remove & place into ice bath until cool.  Drain & pat dry.
In a separate pan, heat 2 Tbsp oil.  Add garlic & the rabe.
Season w/ salt & pepper & saute for 2-3 min.
Add tomato to lentils & cook for 2-3 mi.
Add cream & butter. Stir in basil. Serve w/broccoli on top.
