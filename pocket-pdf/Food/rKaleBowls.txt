Kale Power Bowls

1 cup long grain brown rice $0.40

Vinaigrette
1/4 cup olive oil
3 Tbsp red wine vinegar
1/2 Tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 Tbsp Italian seasoning
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp freshly cracked black pepper

SautÈed Kale
1/2 lb. chopped fresh kale (6 cups)
1/2 Tbsp cooking oil
1 clove garlic, minced
1/8 tsp salt

Bowl Toppings
1 15oz. can cannellini beans
1/4 cup sun dried tomatoes
1/4 cup chopped walnuts
2 oz. feta

1. Cook the rice.
2. Make vinaigrette
3. Saute garlic in oil over medium heat for 1min. Add kale and salt. Saute for a few more minutes until kale is wilted.
4. Prepare rest of toppings. Rinse and drain the beans. Chop sun dried tomatoes and walnuts. Crumble feta.
5. Add 3/4 cup rice to each bowl, 1/2 cup sautÈed kale, 1/3 c beans, and tablespoon each tomatoes and walnuts. Sprinkle a little feta. Add dressing.
