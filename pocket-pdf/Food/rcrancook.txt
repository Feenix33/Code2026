1 cup Butter (cut into cubes)
1 cup + 2 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
2/3 cup Sugar
2 large Eggs
1 teaspoon Vanilla
2 1/4 cup Flour
1 1/2 cup Rolled Oats
1/2 teaspoon Cornstarch
1 teaspoon Baking Soda
1/2 teaspoon Salt
2 cups Sweetened Dried Cranberries
INSTRUCTIONS
 
Oven 365 degrees. 
Cream butter, brown sugar, and sugar.
Add eggs and vanilla and cream for 1 minute
Stir in flour, rolled oats, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt
Fold in dried cranberries
Drop onto a light-colored baking sheet
Bake for 9-11 minutes or until edges starts to turn a light golden color
