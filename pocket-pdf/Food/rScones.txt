Basic Scones
2 c flour
2 tbl sugar + top
2 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 c butter
3/4 c milk
1 egg separated

1 Oven 375
2 Combine flour, sugar, powder, salt
3 Blend in butter until crumb texture
4 Mix milk and yolk, combine w/crumbs
5 Knead 5-6 times 
6 Pat into 7 1/2 round
7 Put on ungreased sheet. Cut through
8 Egg white wash and sprinkle w/sugar
9 Bake 22-25 min

When adding extras, add with milk
1 tsp lemon peel + 1 c walnuts
3/4 c dried currants
