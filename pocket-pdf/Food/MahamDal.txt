Maham's Dal

Dal
1 1/2 c mung dal
1/2 lb tomatoes chopped
3 clove garlic, cushed
1 in ginger, peeld & grated
1/2 t ground turmeric
1 t chile flakes

Spice Oil
3 T canola
10 fresh curry leaves
1 1/2 t salt
1/2 tsp cumin seeds
1 serrano chilie, fine chop
handful fresh cilantro, chopped

1. Put mung dal ingredients in large saucepan w/1T oil, 4 curry leaves, 5 1/4 c water
2. Med heat w/lid ajar to boil, then reduce to simmer 30-40 min, stir occasionally
3. Spiced oil. 2T oil in small fry med heat. Add seeds, chile, remain curry leaves.
4. When leaves crsip up, remove from heat and put into dal.
5. Serve over rice w/cilantro.
