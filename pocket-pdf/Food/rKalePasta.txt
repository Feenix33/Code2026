8 ounces cavatappi pasta
4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
5 cups Nature’s Greens Kale Greens, packed into cup (plus more for sauteing, if desired)
1/2 cup diced sweet yellow onion
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup reserved pasta water
3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
1 lemon, juiced and zested (plus more for garnish)
1/4 teaspoon dijon mustard
1/2 cup unsweetened oat milk
black pepper, to taste

Instructions
1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Reserve at least 1/4 cup water from the cooked pasta.
2. Meanwhile, heat a medium skillet over medium heat. Drizzle with 1 tablespoon olive oil. Add Nature’s Greens kale, diced onion, and salt. Saute until wilted, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in minced garlic. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cover with a lid and cook an additional 5 minutes, until the kale is tender but still vibrant green.
4. Transfer cooked kale and onions to a blender. Add 1/4 cup pasta water, olive oil, 2 tablespoons lemon juice, zest from one lemon, and dijon mustard. Blend until the kale is mostly broken down, then add in the oat milk. Blend until smooth, adding additional oat milk as needed to thin the sauce.
5. Pour the blended kale sauce into a skillet over medium-low heat. Toss with the cooked pasta, additional sauteed kale (optional), and a squeeze of lemon juice. Want to add a protein source? Chickpeas or white beans go well with this dish.
