Strawberry Vinaigrette Recipe

1-2 medium lemons
8 oz fresh strawberries (5-7)
3 Tbl sugar
1 Tbl apple cider vinegar
1/2 tea Dijon mustard
1/2 tea kosher salt
1/3 cup olive oil

Juice lemons until 3 Tbl
Very finely chop strawberries (1/2 cup)
Add 3 Tbl sugar and lightly mash the strawberries
Stir until the sugar dissolves
Add vinegar, mustard, and salt. Whisk until combined
While whisking, slowly drizzle in oil
