The pancake mix box instructions (Aunt Jemima) call for 1 cup of mix, 3/4 cup of milk, 1 tablespoon oil, and 1 egg. Any pancake mix box that has comparable amounts will work.
Prepare pancake mix according to the package directions, but reduce milk or liquid by 1/4 cup.
Stir in one tablespoon of sugar (or more for sweeter donuts).
Spoon the batter into the preheated Donut Maker
You may also preheat the oven to 375 degrees F, lightly spray the donut baking tray with cooking spray, fill the cavities, and bake for about 15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
